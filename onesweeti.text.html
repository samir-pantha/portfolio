"Where Future Leaders Play, Learn & Grow."

Established in 2042, the New Light Secondary School is a public educational trust that makes distinctive contributions to society in research, learning. We strive for quality education.

Our prime focus is on developing knowledgeable, inquiring and caring young people who can help to create a better and more peaceful world. Thus, we strive equipping our young mind with technical, social and academic skills to stand in the global community.
